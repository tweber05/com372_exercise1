Pasta Carbonara

This creamy dish of pasta in a luxurious sauce if bacon, egg, and cheese is a easyto-make treat. Adding lots of good, fresh black pepper will really add to the brightness of the dish and make the other ingredients "pop."

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound of bacon, diced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 pound pasta, cooked al dente
    • The traditional noodle is spaghetti, but linguine also works
    • Don't overcook your noodles! They will become mushy.
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and fresh black pepper, to tatse

Top Three Wines to Pair With Pasta Carbinara

  1. Pinot Noir
  2. Beaujolais Nouveau
  3. Sangiovese

Great Foods to Serve With Pasta Carbonara

  • Good, crusty bread
  • Green salad
  • Olvies
    • Cerignola
    • Kalamata
    • Oil Cured
  • Fried sardines

Directions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a large pan over medium heat until crisp
  2. Remove the bacon from the pan, and pour off all but 2 tablespoons of the rendered fat.
  3. Add the garlic and black pepper to the pan and cook until fragrant, just a minute or so
  4. Add the bacon and pasta back to the pan and cppk another minute.
  5. Take the pan off the heat and add eggs and cheese, stir quickly so the eggs thicken but don't scramble
